The Problem

Constipation Is More Than Just Uncomfortable

Chronic constipation affects millions of people, impacting quality of life, energy levels, and overall health. But most solutions only address the symptoms, not the root cause.

Pelvic Floor Dysfunction

Tight or weak pelvic floor muscles can prevent proper relaxation during bowel movements, leading to chronic straining and incomplete evacuation.

Posture & Alignment Issues

Poor posture and body mechanics can compress the bowel and restrict natural movement, making elimination difficult and painful.

Nervous System Dysregulation

Stress and tension affect the gut-brain connection, disrupting the natural signals that trigger healthy, regular bowel movements.

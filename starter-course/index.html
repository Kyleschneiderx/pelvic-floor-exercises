What You'll Learn

A Complete Pelvic Floor Workout

Everything you need to strengthen, relax, and take control of your pelvic floor — in one comprehensive class.

Pelvic Floor Contractions

Learn the 5 different muscle cues — stop the flow, perineal body, clitoral nod, anal sphincter — so your brain reconnects with each muscle.

Core Activation

Engage your deep abdominal muscles (transverse abdominis) that work together with your pelvic floor for true core stability.

Hip Rotator Exercises

Strengthen the hip rotator cuff — a muscle that sits IN the pelvic floor and is essential for pelvic stability.

Bridge & Glute Work

Single-leg bridges, glute activation, and exercises that build the endurance your pelvic floor needs throughout the day.

Stretches & Relaxation

Essential stretches for tight hip flexors, QL, and the pelvic floor itself — because a tight floor can't function properly.

Breath Work

Learn diaphragmatic breathing that works with your pelvic floor like a piston — essential for pressure management.

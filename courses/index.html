Common Questions

Picking the Right Course

Which course should I start with?

If you're new to pelvic floor training, the Starter Course is the fastest way to build the foundations. If you want a complete, progressive experience, the 8-Week Pelvic Wellness Course is the flagship program and covers the most ground. If you're dealing with a specific issue like bladder leakage or chronic pain, the condition-specific classes are built for that.

Do I get lifetime access?

Yes — every course includes lifetime access. Once you enroll, you can re-watch all lessons anytime, from any device.

Are the courses appropriate for beginners?

Yes. Every course is designed to be accessible to beginners, with clear cues, modifications, and the same teaching style Sheree uses in clinical one-on-one sessions.

What equipment do I need?

Most courses need nothing more than a towel roll, an optional exercise ball, and comfortable floor space. No expensive equipment required.

Can I get a refund if the course isn't right for me?

Please refer to each course's individual page for the most up-to-date refund and support information.
